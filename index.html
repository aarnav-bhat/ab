Jun '25 – Sept '25 Independent Researcher

DNA Methylation Biomarkers for Lung Cancer Detection

Co-authored a peer-reviewed study analyzing 7,240 genomic samples across lung cancer cohorts. Engineered and validated a gradient-boosting ML model achieving AUC 0.983, 92.7% accuracy, and 98.2% recall for early-stage lung cancer screening.

Conducted advanced feature engineering, Cohen's d effect size analysis (p < 1e-70), and cross-validation across diverse demographics. Integrated metabolomic datasets to confirm epigenetic findings.
